Republic Act No. 10173

Data Privacy Act Agreement

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10173, also known as the "Data Privacy Act of 2012", is a landmark legislation in the Philippines that protects the fundamental human right of privacy of communication while ensuring the free flow of information to promote innovation and growth.
