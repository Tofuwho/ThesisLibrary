The Heart of TCU Research

The Taguig City University Thesis Library Archiver is more than just a repository; it's a legacy of city-funded academic brilliance.

Our University Heritage

Established as the premier city university of Taguig, TCU has consistently produced research that addresses local challenges and global demands.

The Digital Archiver was created to ensure that every student's hard work—from the College of Engineering to the Graduate School—is preserved for future generations of Taguigueños. We house over 5,000 academic works, ranging from localized community studies to high-level technological innovations.
