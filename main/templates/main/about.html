The Heart of TCU Research

The Taguig City University Thesis Library Archiver is more than just a repository; it's a digital legacy of city-funded academic brilliance and scientific discovery.

Our University Heritage

Established as the premier city university of Taguig, TCU has consistently produced research that addresses local challenges and global demands.

The Digital Archiver was created to ensure that every student's hard work—from the College of Engineering to the Graduate School—is preserved for future generations of Taguigueños. We house thousands of academic works, ranging from localized community studies to high-level technological innovations.

Through this platform, we bridge the gap between academic theory and practical application, allowing the city government and local industries to leverage the intellect of our brightest minds.

The Archiving Process

How we ensure research quality, metadata compliance, and institutional accessibility.

1

Student Upload

Students fill out research details, keywords, abstracts, co-authors, and upload PDF drafts.

2

Faculty Review

Assigned advisors and review panels examine drafts and add annotations or request updates.

3

Library Curation

Librarians catalog approved works, assign classifications, index departments, and archive files.

4

Digital Access

Approved research becomes instantly indexable, searchable, and previewable by scholars.
